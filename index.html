Our Donate Plan

Our main motive to help the affected people. As for now, our target is to deliver food so that we can at least ensure thier survival. But we have other plans too such as Sheltering, education. Please check our donation plan for more details.

Food for a family

Food is the top most priority fundamental needs for a human being. In any affected area our first concern is to supply enough food for a family, in this plan you will find more details of items of food we distribute during natural disaster.

Shelter for a family

Sheltering plans are divided into twp parts. One is during disaster, another one is after disaster. During disaster, we move the people too high altitude areas where we already built some temporary shelter. And after disaster, we help people to renovate their damages houses.

Education for a child

Education is another fundamental needs for human being. In this plan, we try our best to ensure the proper education for all classes of people. From childer to old people. Sometimes in general situation, we build schools and educational institutions as well
